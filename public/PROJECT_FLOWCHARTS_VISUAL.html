Whole Project Overview

The admin creates the official school and student records. New student records require sex, while each school-year enrollment records whether the student is new, a transferee, or returned. Age is derived from birthdate and legacy missing values display as Pending. Parents can only see students after a student_guardians link exists. One Add students chooser opens focused single-new, batch-new, or existing-student workflows. New-student batches support shared grade, section, and student-type defaults with per-row overrides. Existing students with no enrollment for the selected year use Enroll existing students, which lets the admin select one or many, apply a shared grade/section, review individual placements, and add only enrollment rows without re-entering identity or parent-link data. Parents link existing school-created students instead of enrolling new students directly.

Company Super Admin

The company super admin is an XMETA Pay account, not a school staff account. It signs in at /login, then uses the sidebar-based company workspace to monitor schools and a daily, weekly, monthly, or custom-date school-admin registration trend from /super-admin/dashboard, manage approved school admin accounts from /super-admin/admin-accounts, and review pending school admin registrations from /super-admin/registrations.

Password Recovery

Active company, school-admin, and parent accounts use the recovery link on their matching login page. Unknown or inactive accounts receive the same safe request response without an email.

flowchart TD
  A["Open role-specific Forgot password page"] --> B["Submit account email"]
  B --> C{"Active matching account?"}
  C -->|No| D["Return generic code-sent response"]
  C -->|Yes| E["Store hashed challenge and 6-digit OTP"]
  E --> F["Send OTP through SMTP"]
  F --> G["Verify within 5 minutes and 5 attempts"]
  G --> H["Open 10-minute password reset window"]
  H --> I["Update password, consume challenge, revoke sessions"]
  I --> J["Return to matching login"]

Resend unlocks after 60 seconds and is limited to five sends per account per rolling hour. Password recovery does not change account status.

Tuition terms are implemented as per-student schedules managed from each tuition assignment row. Parents can pay open or partial installments early. The assignment due date is the official parent-facing deadline, and term schedule dates must be on or before that date. Parent payment rows and term rows show the main assignment due date. The parent Fee summary PDF includes nested tuition term rows under each tuition fee. Parents can archive or restore only paid or zero-balance fee rows through parent-specific metadata. Archived rows can also be permanently removed from that parent's lists and exports through a deleted_at tombstone; balances, metrics, school records, admin reports, payment history, receipts, and another guardian's view remain unchanged. Parent Payment history also has parent-specific Current and Archived views for finished payments; pending payments remain visible and receipts, allocations, wallet top-ups, reports, and dashboard totals stay unchanged. Other fees remain on the normal full-balance fee assignment flow.

Payment Reminder History

Implemented: responsive HTML and plain-text reminders with itemized active-year fee balances, official assignment deadlines, optional tuition schedule details, delivery history, and same-day duplicate prevention.

New reminder rows use type = payment_reminder and channel = email. They start as queued, then become sent with sent_at or failed, while message_body keeps the sent text. School administrators and finance officers can archive or restore selected payment-reminder rows through archived_at; delivery status and audit data are retained, and archived sent rows still prevent duplicate same-day emails. Historical SMS rows remain visible. Registrars cannot send or organize reminders. SMS, scheduling, delivery webhooks, bounce handling, and permanent deletion remain future work.
